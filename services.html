§02 Programs

Programs

What Each Class Is For.

Adults · 14+

NoGi Beginners

Zero-experience friendly. We walk you through the key components of submission grappling — positional control, escapes, foundational submissions — in a welcoming, no-ego environment.

Adults · 14+

Jiu-Jitsu (NoGi)

The main adult class. Advanced details, options, chain attacks, and strategic trap-setting. Beginners welcome with a partner or after a few beginners sessions.

Kids · 3–11

Kids Jiu-Jitsu

Age-grouped classes that teach self-defense through our proven system. Whether your kid wants to defend themselves, get in shape, or compete — we've got a path.

All Levels

Open Mat

Wednesdays at 6 PM. Show up, drill with partners, roll, ask questions. The perfect way to absorb what you've been learning at your own pace.
