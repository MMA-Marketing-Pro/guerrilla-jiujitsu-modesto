§01 Welcome

Welcome

Train the art that wins fights, one rep at a time.

The only NoGi-specialty academy in the Modesto area. We teach you how to become an expert at self-defense using Jiu-Jitsu — whether you've never stepped on a mat or you're sharpening for competition.

§02 Programs
Adults · 14+

Adults Jiu-Jitsu (NoGi)

We're the only NoGi-only school in the area. Our beginners program dives into the basic key components and systems of submission grappling. Our advanced class goes deeper into finer details, options, and what to look for when setting traps. Beginner or experienced — both programs are open to you.

Kids · Ages 3–11

Youth / Kids Jiu-Jitsu

Our kids program helps your child become an expert at self-defense using our proven system. Classes for kids age 3 and up, grouped by age for safety. Whether your child wants self-defense, fitness, friends, or to compete — we've got a program that fits.
