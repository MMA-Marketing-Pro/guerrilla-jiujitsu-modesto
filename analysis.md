# Guerrilla Jiu-Jitsu Modesto — Analysis

## Business
Modesto-based BJJ academy specializing in **NoGi** grappling — adults and kids (age 3+). Owner/Head Instructor: Mike Cruz, black belt under Alejandro Perez, mentored by Dave Camarillo. Positioned as "The #1 Jiu-Jitsu Academy in Modesto, CA" with a family-friendly, beginner-welcoming tone.

## Brand Vibe
Modern combat-sport with an accessible community feel — not pro-fighter-roster intense, not legacy-heritage. Strong lineage credentials but marketed to everyday people (parents, beginners, kids).

## Design Recommendation
**`performance-athletic-skill`** — bold sportswear-brand energy, kinetic marquees, one electric accent. Rationale:
- Modern/accessible/family-friendly positioning (NOT pro fighter roster → not cinematic-fight)
- No multi-decade heritage signals → not championship-heritage
- No S&C/metric-driven positioning → not training-lab
- Best general-purpose pick per CLAUDE.md for combat-sports clients like this

Use brand colors: gold **#AE9958** (primary accent) + gunmetal gray **#2A2D31** (secondary) on near-black base **#1A1C1F**. Gold is the single electric accent — gunmetal is the structural mid-tone (cards, borders, secondary surfaces).
