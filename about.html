Owner / Head Instructor

Mike Cruz

Mike has spent the last nine years chasing the details of Jiu-Jitsu at the highest level. A black belt under Alejandro Perez and a student of legendary coach Dave Camarillo, Mike built Guerrilla Jiu-Jitsu Modesto to bring that same obsessive attention to detail to students in the 209.

Why NoGi Only

Modern Jiu-Jitsu. Closer to Reality.

We're the only NoGi-specialty school in the area — and that's intentional. NoGi forces you to rely on leverage, timing, and body control instead of grips that only exist on a training floor. It translates faster to self-defense and moves at the tempo that modern competition rewards.

Whether you plan to compete, defend yourself, or just train hard with good people — NoGi sharpens what matters.
